About

Not long ago, high-end motion picture finishing required expensive turn-key systems and huge overheads, but not anymore.

Here, your work won't be forced through a cookie-cutter pipeline. Meeting technical specifications should be a given, not the end game — I'm passionate about using the finishing process to tastefully enhance your storytelling.

The Finishing Suite is conveniently right in the heart of Ponsonby — just two minutes walk from Ponsonby Central.

Alastair Tye Samson
Finishing Artist

For fifteen years I have worked as an Online Editor, Colourist, and all-round motion picture finishing nerd.

I offer inspiration and experience using Da Vinci Resolve, Autodesk Smoke, and Avid Media Composer to finesse films, television shows, music videos and TVCs to be more awesome. I am fast and meticulous with an up-to-date knowledge of technology and a strong creative eye.

Over my 20 years experience in the screen industries I have worked with many high profile companies such as Weta Digital, Eyeworks Touchdown, Images&Sound, South Pacific Pictures, NHNZ and heaps of others.

I love stories and storytelling. In 2009 I co-founded Hybrid Motion Pictures, a filmmaking collective with whom I produced and self-distributed my first indie feature film, JAKE, which was released theatrically in 2014.
